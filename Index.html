Blockchain Implementation in Supply Chain Management

With blockchain's inherent ability to trace drug provenance and create an immutable record of the lifecycle of a drug and how it was handled, this open industry initiative addresses the challenges the pharmaceutical industry faces, both in sourcing and distributing drugs.
